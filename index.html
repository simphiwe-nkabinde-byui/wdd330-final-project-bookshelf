Welcome

Welcome to Bookshelf. Bookshelf is a web application that helps users manage information about books that they are interested in. Users can also record notes about books they have listed on the app. This application is targeted at anyone who reads published books.

Go to My Bookshelf

Features

You can enjoy the following features on Bookshelf

  • Search for your favourite books
  • View results from your search
  • View details about books you've searched or saved
  • Add Books to your reading, completed or wishlist category
  • Update the books stored in your categories. Move them to a different category or remove them from your saved books
